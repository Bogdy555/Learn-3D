Shading

Introduction

There were a few techniques over the years to shade 3D models. I would like to show them all and present some differences.

1. Phong shading

This is one of the first ones that was quite widly spread and used. It is a crude aproximation, but it gave good enought results.

2. Blinn-Phong shading

The Phong aproach was quite flawed when it came to specular lighting on small angles, so it was later improved to Blinn-Phong

3. PBR with IBL shading

PBR is the best that we got so far. As the name suggests, it is physically based. It respects the laws of conservation, the microfacet model and the Fresnel effect.
